### [Modern Cryptography](/posts/2017/2017-06-04-cryptography.md)

"The enciphering and deciphering of messages in secret code or cipher; also :  the computerized encoding and decoding of information" - [Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/cryptography). Enigma, hashing, and the Caesar Cipher are all examples of cryptography.
